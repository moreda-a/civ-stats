About This Page

I created this page for fun and to better analyze statistics of Civilization VI multiplayer games. I hope you'll find it useful as well! All data comes from the CivPlayers League (CPL) Discord, which hosts thousands of multiplayer games per month.

  • Monthly Stats: Includes all FFA (Free For All) matches with 10 players for the month of September.
  • Top 100 Players: Displays stats for the current top 100 pro players over the last 70 days.
  • Easy Lobby (Pro): Shows data for matches where high-rated players were expected to win, covering the last 70 days.
  • Hard Lobby (Noob): Highlights games where underrated players faced stronger opponents, based on data for the last 70 days.
  • Teamers Stats: Displays team game results over the last 70 days.

Number of wins means First Place. and Average ranking is the Average rank of specific civ-leader in all counted games(For example, rank is a number between 1 to 10 for FFA).

Use the menu at the top to navigate through the stats. Each page allows sorting by various criteria for a better analysis experience.
