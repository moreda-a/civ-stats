About This Page

This website provides statistical analysis of players(in the future) and civilizations in the Civilization VI multiplayer games. All the data is sourced from the CivPlayers League(CPL) Discord, which hosts thousands of multiplayer games per month.

Monthly stats include all FFA (Free For All) matches with 10 players. For September.

The top 100 pages include the stat of games of the current top 100(pro) players over the last 40 days.

Easy lobbies(Pro) consider matches for people with higher ratings than the rest of the lobby people who are expected to win the lobby. Data is for the last 40 days.

Data about underrated players in a lobby against stronger players called hard lobby(Noob). Data is gathered over the last 40 days.

Last, you can see the results of teamers over 40 days.

To navigate between the different statistics, use the menu at the top of the page. Also, you can sort based on different criteria on each page.
